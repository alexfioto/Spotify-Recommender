Create Playlist Based on Artist

This application creates a playlist of a specific artist that is based off of your personal taste. To find a spotify artist URI, you will need to go to the spotfiy desktop app. Right click on the artist. Hover over share and then click "Copy Spotify URI". Paste this into the cell below.





If you get a 500 error, try changing some of the parameters or artist. This is a free server so sometimes it does get overloaded. I'm still working out the kinks. Thank you for your patience and hope you find some good tunes!
